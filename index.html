DSLR CAMERA

A proper camera body which contains a mirror that reflects the light coming from the lens up into an optical viewfinder. You are able to see your image in real-time and the quality of output will be much higher.

COMPACT CAMERA

It has fixed lens directly built into the camera body. Can be referred to as point and shoot as all you have to do is aim and press the button while the camera itself does the rest automatically.
